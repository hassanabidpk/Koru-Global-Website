KoRu Global – assistance in organization of language trainings in Korea

All major universities in South Korea offer language programs for foreigners, willing to learn Korean. Being on a college campus, it is easy for foreign students to make friends with Korean students and learn Korean faster. Diverse programs are tailored to suit the needs of students: schools offer different curriculum, living conditions and tuition fees. Tell us necessary details and we will offer a best language school optimal for you. KoRu offers following services in organization of language trainings in Korea:

>> Free consultation on language programs and schools
>> Documents preparation in the school – documents collection, application and registration.
>> Sending invitation to the student – when admission is confirmed, we prepare all documents and send to you to get visa.
>> Accommodation and other details assistance – depending on your needs, suitable accommodation options will be provided.
>> Meeting in the airport – if necessary, you will be picked up in the airport and attended on the way to the school.
>> Assistance during study – we also provide assistance during student’s study in Korea, such as foreigner registration or visa extension, and also help to solve any other issues.

Curriculum

Korean language is divided in 6 levels depending on speaker's proficiency, starting from 1 – beginner level. Language schools offer 4 semesters with different levels per year. Each semester is 2,5 month long and include morning or day classes. After exams, students are allowed to continue on the next level. Also, depending on your needs, it is possible to organize short-term trainings for 3-5 weeks or summer language camps.

Short term programs

Short term programs are offered every month. They include approximately 5 hours of classes every day and take from 3 to 5 weeks. Tuition fee is around 700$.

Information about visa

Students from some countries may be exempt from visas under national agreements if taking one semester only or arrive by tourist visa. Studying for more than one semester requires D-4 visa – student short-term visa. Documents: passport, visa application, A graduation (or enrollment) certificate of the most recently attended school, documents proving financial stability, invitation letter from Korean school. * Additional documents may be requested upon need.


KoRu Global will assist you to ensure successful process of application and admission, as well as provide help during you stay in Korea.
