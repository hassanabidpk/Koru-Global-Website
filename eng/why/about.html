KORU Global - Global Expert in International Trade

KORU Global is a trading company providing direct
access to producers of any goods and services in
South Korea. Years of experience in international
trade made KORU a recognized expert of Korean
market.

Slogan of our company is “Passion for Excellence with Every Client!” Multilanguage managers quickly conduct search for any product in South Korea with good quality and reasonable price, which would fit personal inquiries of our clients.

The goal of KORU Global – to develop a global one-stop platform, with full international trade services in South Korea. We want to provide the most comfortable conditions of product supply for our clients. Full range of KORU trade services include, but not limited to:

1) Search of direct producers in South Korea;
2) Negotiations and representation of client’s company;
3) Personal guidance in Korea;
4) Export-import documents preparations;
5) Work with Customs procedures;
6) Notarization of trade documents;
7) Money remittance in/out of Korea;
8) International shipment of goods;
9) Assistance in product certification in client’s country.


We also want to promote cultural and society interaction between Korea and other countries by providing tourism and education support programs in South Korea.

KORU Global invites everyone who is interested in our service for mutually beneficial cooperation, regardless of your location and company size!
