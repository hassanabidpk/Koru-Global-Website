KoRu Global provides international logistics services with discount to its clients

Wherever you are, delivery of goods to your country from South Korea is not a problem with KoRu Global.

We not only assist in product search in Korea but also offer best solutions for international shipment of your goods. All necessary logistics services with discount rates will be provided once our clients are ready to purchase any goods.

According to client’s requirements, we will provide following international logistics services:

>> Warehousing handling;
>> Packing solutions;
>> Full and Part loads;
>> Airline, Steam Line choice;
>> Inland Trucking;
>> Oversize Cargo handling;
>> Insurance;
>> Broker’s support.

Personal manager is in charge of every client’s shipment item to provide the fastest service. Once we received inquiry for any product in Korea, we will automatically calculate shipping costs according to the size of shipment, client’s destination and type of delivery. On average, shipment of large consignments takes from 10 to 40 days.

EMS Express Postal Service

We can also organize an express parcel delivery up to 30 kg. by EMS. As soon as client’s goods are received, the manager will examine quality and amount after which the consignment of goods will be prepared for sending by EMS.
